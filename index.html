FYP Suggestion System through AI

For my final year project, I designed an intelligent suggestion system using artificial intelligence to assist students in selecting their final year projects (FYP). The system analyzes students' interests, academic performance, and trending topics in technology to provide personalized project recommendations. By employing natural language processing and collaborative filtering techniques, the application not only improves the selection process but also fosters innovation by connecting students with relevant and exciting project ideas. This project demonstrates the practical application of AI in education, making project selection more efficient and tailored to individual preferences.
